Settings

Your key is stored only in this browser and sent to this Echo server, which relays your requests to Anthropic on your behalf. It is not stored server-side or sent anywhere else.

Transcription

Local Whisper transcribes the video's audio on-device when YouTube captions are missing (Fallback) or always, instead of relying on captions (High-accuracy). Nothing is uploaded — it runs entirely on your machine. Local/desktop only.

Markdown vault

Sync your whole library into an Obsidian vault — an Obsidian vault is just a folder of Markdown files. Pick an existing vault folder, or create a new (empty) folder to start one.

Writes each library entry as a Markdown file into this folder on your machine.

Or download your whole library as a Markdown ZIP.

Used by the per-note "Send to Obsidian" button on each saved entry. Leave blank to use whichever vault is currently open in the Obsidian app.
